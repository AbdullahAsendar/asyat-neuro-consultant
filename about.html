ABOUT ANC SERVICES

ANC Services:

Dedicated to your Child’s Growth

We are dedicated to making each child reach their full potential at the clinic. Our highly passionate group of specialists are behavior analysts who have achieved their Behavior Analysis Certification Board (BACB), approved through graduate programs. They are certified board behavior analysts. Many of these analysts have acquired their Master’s degree in Psychology with a specialization in Applied Behavior Analysis. We pick every individual within our team based upon their qualifications, methods approach, experience and amount of education. Our team of committed and passionate members will ensure that the experience with in-home therapy is a positive experience for everyone.

An All-Encompassing In-Home

ABA Therapy Service

It is our duty to provide your child with an environment where he or she can focus on learning. A child needs this type of environment in order to facilitate his or her skills set. Within this environment are the home caregivers and family as well. This is why we empower them to participate in therapy sessions so they can learn as well. Our ABA Therapist and behavior analyst are there to teach you with the evidence-based procedures/methods that will prepare you to respond to behaviours when the staff isn’t present. Furthermore, to family training, we also take care of your benefits and coverage checks while communicating them directly to you. Our organization maintained strong relationships with many of the health insurance companies. This lets us ease the burden of administrative tasks on the family in order to focus on the child’s progress instead.
