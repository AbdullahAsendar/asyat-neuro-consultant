CAREERS

ABA Therapy and BCBA Career Opportunities in Phoenix and Scottsdale, Arizona

At Advanced Autism Services, we are committed to hiring passionate, qualified therapists and BCBAs to join our team and provide the highest level of in-home care to our clients in the Phoenix and Scottsdale area(s). We pride ourselves in offering a career-oriented work environment where our team members can grow and learn in their field. ABA Therapy and BCBA roles offer rewarding work that improves the lives of children living with Autism Spectrum Disorder. ABA Therapy and BCBA staff can choose from full- time or part-time work, making this opportunity perfect for those seeking flexible work hours. If you are a qualified ABA Therapy professional or a BCBA in the Phoenix or Scottsdale area looking to take your career to the next level, we encourage you to reach out to us and apply to one of our current open positions!

Board Certified Behavior Analyst Positions

Phoenix/Scottsdale

Currently hiring for Full-time and Part-Time BCBAS in the Phoenix, Scottsdale area. We offer competitive hourly pay based on education and experience. We offer training resources, a career advancement path, opportunities to work with and learn from other BCBAs on our staff, flexible hours for part-time work, Full-time benefits such as 401K, Health insurance and more.

Requirements: We are seeking dedicated professionals who are Board Certified Behavior Analysts with a Master’s in ABA or a related field. Must be within a 10-mile radius of Scottsdale, Arizona or Phoenix, Arizona and be willing to travel to patients’ homes to work with kids one-on-one.

Job Description – BCBAs at Advanced Autism Services Will:

  • Conduct assessments and provide progress reports periodically.
  • Monitor the child’s behaviors and environment and formulate a behavior care plan that will be enacted by the assigned ABA Therapist.
  • Supervise, train and provide direction for the ABA Therapists.
  • Develop the procedures for any behavior interventions that may be needed while providing support, guidance and feedback to the child’s family and caregivers.
  • Perform functional assessments and analysis of any behavioral challenges
  • Measure the program’s success, provide recommendations, monitor data collection and progress

ABA Behavior Therapist Positions

Phoenix/Scottsdale

Currently hiring for Full-time and Part-Time ABA Behavioral Therapists in the Phoenix, Scottsdale area to join our team. We offer a supportive work environment for ABA Therapists looking to advance in their career, competitive pay, flexibility in work schedules, career advancement opportunities, a rewarding line of work and a network of passionate ABA and BCBA professionals to grow with and learn from.

Requirements: We are hiring individuals who have working experience with kids for at least a year in a one-on-one setting. Candidates must have excellent verbal and written communication skills. Those applying for the ABA Therapist role must have reliable means of transportation to arrive at clients’ home as needed.

Job Description – ABA Behavior Therapists Will:

  • Work together with their assigned BCBA who supervises their work to apply basic principles and teaching methods in line with ABA Therapy and the child’s care plan.
  • Be punctual and prepared for all session appointments.
  • Work one-on-one with children in their homes, providing In-Home Applied Behavioral Analysis Therapy.
  • Observe and collect behavior and skill acquisition information during sessions and communicate it with the assigned supervising BCBA.
  • Provide support, guidance and feedback to the child’s family and caregivers.
  • Work towards goals identified in the child’s behavioral care plan, implement and provide insight and guidance for the family and/or caregivers.
